Our Story

Hey there, my name is Wes Gates. I created Eydev web solutions back in 2013 because I noticed a trend in small business websites; they weren't good. And, on top of that, they were expensive. I hate seeing small businesses get taken advantage of by web agencies that over-promise and under-deliver. Most businesses don't need a website with all of the bells and whistles that will cost them too much. Eydev's pricing model was built with this in mind. That's why we will build your companies website for $0 down and only $150 a month.

At Eydev we focus on the unique needs of each your business and tailor content around that. Our process is extremly collaborative, we work closely with you to create a website you're thrilled with and not something you just settle for.
